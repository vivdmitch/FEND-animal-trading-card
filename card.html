The Mighty Giraffe!

With an average height of 15-20 feet, the giraffe is the tallest mammal on earth.

  • Scientific Name: Giraffa camelopardalis
  • Average Weight: 1800-2600 lbs
  • Average Lifespan: 25 years
  • Habitat: Savannas, grasslands, or woodlands

With their graceful necks and patchwork coats, giraffes are one of the animal kingdom's most beautiful giants. Newborn giraffes tower over most humans, and baby giraffes are able to run only ten hours after birth. Even a giraffe's mouth is huge! The average length of its distinctive black tongue is 18 inches.
